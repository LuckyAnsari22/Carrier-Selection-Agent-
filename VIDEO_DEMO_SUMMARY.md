## 🎥 RECORDING BEST PRACTICES

1. **Always move slowly** - Give viewers time to see each element
2. **Pause after clicking** - Wait 1 second before speaking
3. **Let pages load** - Don't rush, show the loading state
4. **Speak clearly** - Enunciate, pause between sentences
5. **Use natural inflection** - Don't sound robotic
6. **Look at the right things** - Point with mouse cursor
7. **Keep it professional** - Smooth movements, no jerky motions
